      How to Play

      1. Select a board-size
      2. Select a category
        Categories are fetched from the database based on the selected board size.
      3. Click on a letter to start a word
        Clicking on a letter will add it to the current word.
      4. Click on the next letter
        Letters can only be added to the current word if they are adjacent to the last letter added. This includes diagonals. To remove a letter, you can click on the last letter of the word that was added or click on the "Clear Word" button.
      5. Finish the word
        Clicking on the last letter of a valid word submits it. If the word is valid, it will be crossed off the word bank and the score will be updated.
      6. Submit Score
        Once the game is over, you can submit your score to the database. This will reset the game.
